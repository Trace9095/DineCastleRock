## Tone of Voice
Warm, appetizing, and community-oriented. Dine Castle Rock celebrates the local dining scene with enthusiasm — from craft breweries and family restaurants to fine dining and food trucks. The voice is that of a trusted local foodie who knows every hidden gem. Inviting and descriptive, making readers hungry to explore.

## Target Audience
Castle Rock residents and visitors looking for dining experiences. Demographics: 25-55, mix of families, date-night couples, and foodies exploring the local restaurant scene. Secondary: restaurant owners managing their listings. Day-trippers from Denver/Colorado Springs.

## Content Themes
- Restaurant spotlights and new openings
- Cuisine guides by type (Mexican, Italian, BBQ, etc.)
- Happy hour and special event roundups
- "Best of Castle Rock" dining lists
- Local chef and food scene stories
